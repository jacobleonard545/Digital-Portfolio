Projects


STEAM: A Club for STEM and Arts Students

Students in STEM and Arts lack in collaborating with one another as their projects in college revolve around working with team members of similar backgrounds. This is a problem as students enter the work force and aren't prepared to work with others with a different mindset. I discuss the divide between the two with students both in the STEM and Arts fields as well as possible solutions in a 15-minute podcast.

Violence in Film

Violence in movies and television have an underlying effect on audiences that we may not realize as some films have had a direct influence behinds crimes as terrible as murder. I worked with a team to address the effects of crimes that were inspired by some films as well as studies that give evidence of increased aggression behind these types of media. We created a website to spread awareness and address how we can take action to prevent such heinous acts from happening again.
